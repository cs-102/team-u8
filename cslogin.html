Central Library IIT Goa is a member of DELNET (Developing Library Network), New Delhi. As a member, the research community of IIT Goa can utilize the services and databases of DELNET. The most exciting feature is access to millions of Networked Library Resources.Users can catch a glimpse of the services provided by DELNET as below:

  • Resource discovery through DELNET databases
  • Inter Library Loan & Document Delivery Services
  • Reference Services
  • Resources sharing among all the Networked Libraries through DELNET
